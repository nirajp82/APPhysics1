# 🎯 AP Physics 1 + Math Foundation Plan (Oct 2025 – Jan 2026)

**Goal:** Build strong Precalculus, Trigonometry, and Conceptual Calculus foundations to master AP Physics 1 by January 2026.  
**Study Time:** 15–20 min on weekdays, 1–2 hours on weekends.  
**Focus:** Quality understanding over quantity — integrate math with physics every week.

---
